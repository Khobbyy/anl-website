Features & Services

Art and Leisure-EB provides reliable and affordable world-class services.

High Speed Wifi

Our Wi-Fi service is reliable, secure and available through out the hotel so you can browse the web, stay connected with loved ones and get work done without any interruptions.

Workspace

Our rooms have a dedicated workspace designed to cater for the needs of business travelers, digital nomads and anyone else who needs a quiet, exotic, serene and comfortable place to work.

Art Gallery

With on-site art gallery, guests can visit and purchase unique pieces, each telling a story of our Ghanaian heritage and culture. Our gallery is sure to captivate and inspire you during your stay at Art and Leisure-EB.

Food & Art Experiential

Guests will have access to training experience in traditional ghanaian food and beverage preparation and services. In addition guests can have a feel of art and craft design and production.

Public Facilities Nearby

There's a swimming beach close to the facility along side a fishing community / harbour where guests can have access to a traditional community living at Art and Leisure-EB Hotel.

Bar

We have bar and dinning that serves traditional ghanaian, continental and oriental foods and beverages.
