Art and Leisure-EB is based along the coastal belt of Sekondi-Takoradi, Western Region Ghana. We are about 15 minutes away from the Takoradi Airport or the city mall and 40 minutes from Elimina. We provide welcoming and serene atmosphere for both long and short term stays.
Our facility is designed to cater for the needs of holiday makers, wedding guests, funeral guests, researchers and others from all over the world whether you're here for business or leisure. The facility is owned by art/craft and hospitality practitioners and educators.

Currently we have one executive suite(two bedrooms and a hall), a family suite made up of three bedrooms and a hall, three deluxe rooms, two standard rooms and three budget rooms.Both the executive and family suite can be occupied by four persons respectively.
The deluxe and the standard rooms can take up to two occupants. We also offer group stays if needed two or more apartments could be joined for large group stays. Currently our facility can host up to 20 guests including children. We are disabled and eco-friendly.

At the facility we also offers other services such as:

  • Art and craft production and sales.
  • Food/fruit & vegetables craft and services.
  • Event organizing.
  • Training programmes on Art/Craft and Hospitality.
